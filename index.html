WHAT'S CORONA VIRUS ?

COVID-19

COVID-19 is the disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new visus and disease were unknown before the outbreak beganin Wuhan, China in December 2019.

Corona Virus are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in humans or animals. The new coronavirus can be spread from person to person. It is diagnosed with a laboratory test. At this time, there are no specific vaccines or treatments for COVID-19. However, there are many ongoing clinical trials evaluating potential treatments. WHO will continue to provide updated information as soon as clinical findings become available.

Symptoms

6 Steps Prevention Against Covid-19

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap or alcohol base hand sanitizer

Cover your nose and mouth with mask specialy when you sneeze or cough.

Avoid close contact with people who are unwell. maintain social distance.

Stay home and self-isolated if you feel unwell.

Stay informed by watching news. And follow Goverment Guidlines.

If you have fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. Seek Medical care early.
